About Us

When it comes to crafting luxurious bathroom spaces, Bathrooms and More, LLC leads the way. As a family-owned business based in Great Falls, MT, we specialize in designing and constructing high-end, custom walk-in showers. Our unique installer-direct service model eliminates middlemen, providing a more efficient and cost-effective experience for our clients.

We blend over two decades of industry experience with our passion for superior service, exceptional craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to transform your ordinary bathroom into a luxurious haven of style, comfort, and elegance, one shower at a time.

We hold fast to our commitment to excellence, using only the highest quality materials and aligning with industry-leading brands for our installations. At Bathrooms and More, every detail counts. Your luxury bathroom isn't just a room - it's a lifestyle statement that we help you make.

Our Services

We pride ourselves on our bespoke service, crafting custom walk-in showers that blend luxury with functionality. Bathrooms and More goes beyond providing a service - we offer a seamless experience that transforms your bathroom into your personal sanctuary.

What We Offer:

  • Design Services: We kick-start the transformation with a deep dive into your vision and preferences. Then we craft a custom shower design that embodies luxury, functionality, and style, enhancing the overall appeal of your bathroom.
  • Installation Services: Our unique installer-direct service model ensures a smooth and efficient installation. Our expert installers handle your project personally, resulting in an efficient, cost-effective, and satisfying experience.
  • Material Selection: We hand-pick a range of high-end materials for your installations. From natural stone and Fibo wall panels to fixtures from renowned brands, we ensure that your bathroom embodies luxury and durability.
  • Consultation and Advice: Our team is always available to guide you through the selection of materials, design decisions, and maintenance. Our goal is to make sure you're well-informed and delighted with the choices made for your space.
