Our Services

At Bathrooms and More, we specialize in the design and installation of custom residential walk-in showers. Our services cater to those seeking an elegant balance between functionality and aesthetic appeal. Design Services: Our design process starts with understanding your vision, style preferences, and functional needs. With meticulous planning, we create custom shower designs that embody luxury and practicality while also enhancing the overall appeal of your bathroom space. Installation Services: We manage the full installation process, from preparing the bathroom area to installing the necessary fixtures and fittings. Our experts ensure a smooth and efficient installation, with minimal disruption to your home. Material Selection: We work with a range of high-end materials such as natural stone, Fibo wall panels, porcelain or ceramic tiles, glass panels, solid surface materials, stainless steel panels, and acrylic panels. We also collaborate with renowned brands like Kohler, Porcelanosa, Caesarstone, Dornbracht, Duravit, and Wetstyle for fixtures and accessories. Consultation and Advice: We provide expert advice and consultation at every step. Our team assists in material selection, design decisions, and maintenance advice, ensuring you're well-informed and satisfied with the choices made. With Bathrooms and More, you can be confident of a seamless service experience that transforms your bathroom into a sanctuary of luxury and comfort.
